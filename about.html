Born in Paris, Made to Restore

Apothecary Re-Imagined

Founded in 2021 by clinical biochemists and botanical researchers, LUMINA was created as a direct reaction against the trend of over-exfoliation and harsh, multi-step acid routines that compromise the natural acid mantle.

We believe skin health resides in simplicity. Our lab isolates high-purity active compounds from traditional botanical ingredients, stabilizing them inside lipid-matching formulas to mimic your skin's raw cellular blueprint.

We do not seek to mask; we seek to restore your barrier's original, self-defending hydration cycle.

Dermal Eco-Equity

The Circular Packaging Charter

Our commitment to your skin is inseparable from our responsibility to the planet. Beauty shouldn't be landfill debris.

Every bottle we formulate is protected in heavyweight pharmaceutical glass that blocks UV rays natively (no chemical shields needed) and is completely recyclable. Our caps and pumps are constructed from recycled post-consumer plastics, minimizing virgin resource drains.

Moreover, we source our botanicals from ethical, rain-harvested cooperatives, ensuring fair-wage equity to farming hands in botanical hotspots globally.
